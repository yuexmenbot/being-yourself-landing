How This Works

This is body-based presence work—not traditional talk therapy or cognitive strategies.

Through somatic inquiry, we work directly with your body to:

  • Process what's stored in your body (including past trauma) through felt experience, not just talking about it
  • Reconnect you with your emotions, sensations, and natural state of mind
  • Shift survival-driven patterns (control, struggle, disconnection) into grounded presence

The work is tailored to your process—it could be coaching, it could be somatic therapy. What it's not: clinical diagnosis or traditional cognitive therapy.

Common Questions

Is this therapy?
It's tailored to you. It could be coaching or somatic therapy, depending on what you need. I don't do clinical diagnosis, but I do work with trauma through body-based methods.

What about past trauma?
I absolutely work with past trauma—but through somatic experiencing, not just talking. We process it through your body, where it's actually stored.

How is this different from regular therapy?
This is body-based, not mind-based. The opposite of cognitive behavioral therapy. We work with what you feel, not what you think.

How long does this take?
We start with a free 1.5-hour discovery call. From there, most clients work with me for 3-6 months.
